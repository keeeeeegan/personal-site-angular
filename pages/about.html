About Me

I'm Keegan Berry, a web developer and designer in Orlando, Florida. I'm passionate about creating websites and interfaces, designs, and illustrations.

I generally work with Javascript (Angular and jQuery, for the most part), X/HTML, and CSS when working on websites and user interfaces. For graphics and illustrations, I work in Adobe products, including Illustrator and Photoshop, but I usually always start on paper. I have the an attention to detail (but that can sometimes be a curse) and I enjoy working with pixel-precision. While meeting clients' needs, I always strive to learn something new, have fun, and improve my craft of creating robust, intuitive, and eye-catching work.

I enjoy doodling in my spare time...if I'm not watching a movie or practicing my culinary skills, that is. I also like going out and exploring, taking photos, and trying one of the thousand new bars / coffee shops in Orlando.
